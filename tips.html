Sustainable Eating Tips

1. Buy Local and Seasonal

Choose fruits and vegetables that are in season and grown locally to reduce transportation emissions.

2. Reduce Meat Consumption

Try having meatless meals a few times a week. Plant-based proteins have a lower carbon footprint.

3. Minimize Food Waste

Plan your meals, store food properly, and use leftovers creatively to reduce waste.

4. Choose Sustainable Packaging

Opt for products with minimal or recyclable packaging. Bring reusable bags when shopping.

5. Grow Your Own Food

Start a small herb garden or grow vegetables at home to reduce your environmental impact.

6. Compost Food Scraps

Turn food waste into nutrient-rich compost for your garden instead of sending it to landfills.
